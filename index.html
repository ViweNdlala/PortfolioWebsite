Hi, I am Viwe Ndlala, a final year Computer Science and Business Computing student at University Of Cape Town. Passionate about creating solutions that address real-world challenges.

My academic journey of computer science and business computing has exposed me in programming and a clear understanding of information systems (work systems). Both these play a significant role in facilitating operational efficiencies and service delivery to end users.

I am equipped with Business process redesign, Systems analysis, Design and Development, and programming knowledge moslty in Java and Python. Familiar with C# and Databases (SQL). My field of study has developed an interest to be in an environment that involves continuous and holistically identification of challenges, customer needs, and creation of ideas intended to solve them. I have embarked on the journey of Design Thinking which I found resonating with me because of its human-centred, curiosity and core problem identification nature.

Participated in a Design Thinking week for my INF3011F course and another one on my own at Hasso Pattner d-school Afrika. This depeened my empathy, user-centred mindset, and creative problem solving skills. During these programmes I learnt how to understand user needs, reframe problems, ideate innovative solutions, and prototype quickly.

Currently on a journey to become a fullstack developer. I have built basic skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design with media queries. I am also learning backend technologies and database design to build complete applications. My goal is to create software systems that integrate frontend user experiences with backend logic to solve problems effectively.

I am driven by a desire to solve any life challenges which create obstacles to goals. As I continue to grow in this field, I look forward to identify core problems and developing solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and communities.
